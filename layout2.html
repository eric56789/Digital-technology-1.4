What cause Weka endanger

The world is dealing with an unprecedented threat to wildlife. Farming, technology development and mining have dramatically effected Weka. The effects are catastrophic, each year, there are 5,000 Weka killed. Now, there are only around 40,000 Weka left. Scientist estimate Weka will be extinct in 2030.
